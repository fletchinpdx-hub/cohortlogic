Our pricing commitments

How we'll handle pricing when it changes.

We'll be transparent about the "why" when pricing changes, and we'll structure it so that the schools that can't afford it still have access to the core tools they need.

📣

Advance notice

We'll announce any pricing changes at least 90 days before they take effect. You'll never be surprised by a charge. We'll tell you what's changing and why.

🏫

School-sized pricing

When we introduce paid tiers, they'll be priced for school budgets — not district enterprise contracts. A single school should be able to afford this without a procurement process.

🔓

Free tier stays

A meaningful free tier will always exist. Core functionality — import, balance, export — will not go behind a paywall. We may charge for advanced features, but not the basics.

📤

Your data, always

You can export everything at any time. No data lock-in, no hostage files. If you stop using Cohort Logic, you take your work with you.

🤝

Title I consideration

We'll maintain a program for Title I schools that provides full access at no cost. The schools that need these tools most will always be able to get them.

💬

Users help set the price

When we're ready to launch paid tiers, we'll talk to active users first. Pricing will reflect what's actually worth paying for in your workflow — not what a consultant thinks.

Why free right now?

We're building in public with real schools.

Class Builder is in its early phase. We want real administrators using it with real rosters so we can learn what works and what doesn't. Free access gets it into the hands of the people who can tell us the most. It's that simple.

When it's ready to charge for, it'll be because we've built something genuinely worth paying for.
