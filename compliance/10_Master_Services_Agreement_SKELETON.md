## Master Services Agreement — outline

1. **Parties & effective date** — Cohort Logic (LLC — confirm exact entity, BI-01) and the District/School ("Customer").
2. **Definitions** — Service, Student Data, Personal Data, Authorized Users, Order, Sub-processor, etc.
3. **Services & orders** — the products licensed (Class Builder / CICO / Schedule Builder / Referral Tracking), provisioning, and how Orders/quotes attach.
4. **License & access** — right to access the Service for the Customer's internal educational use; Authorized Users; restrictions (mirror the AUP).
5. **Fees & payment** — subscription fees, term of pricing, invoicing, taxes, late payment. [Coordinate with pricing.html + Stripe, PR-01/09.]
6. **Term & termination** — initial term, renewal, termination for cause/convenience, effect of termination.
7. **Data protection (core)** — incorporates the **DPA/NDPA** (Exhibit 1); FERPA "school official" designation and direct-control terms; purpose limitation; **no sale / no advertising / no secondary use**; sub-processor list; security controls; **breach notification** (≤72h default, ≤7 days hard ceiling per `05_...`); **data return & deletion** on termination; assistance with parent/student rights.
8. **Confidentiality** — mutual; carve-outs; Student Data always treated as Customer confidential information.
9. **Intellectual property** — Cohort Logic owns the Service; Customer owns its data; feedback license.
10. **Warranties** — authority; service performed with reasonable skill; Customer's authority to provide Student Data.
11. **Indemnification** — Cohort Logic for IP/data-security breaches (attorney to scope); Customer for unauthorized data or misuse.
12. **Limitation of liability** — cap + exclusions; **carve-outs likely for data-breach/confidentiality** (districts often require a higher cap or carve-out here — coordinate with cyber-insurance limits, BI-04/05).
13. **Insurance** — Cohort Logic maintains cyber liability and technology E&O at stated limits; provides a COI on request (BI-04/05/07). *(Districts frequently mandate this clause and specific limits.)*
14. **Compliance** — FERPA, COPPA, and applicable state student-privacy laws; cooperation with the Customer's compliance obligations.
15. **General** — assignment, notices, **governing law/venue = State of Oregon** (set 2026-07-24, Doc 20 Section A; attorney to confirm), force majeure, entire agreement, order of precedence (DPA controls for Student Data).
    - **Notice address (Provider):** Cohort Logic, LLC, 9809 SE Nicholas Drive, Happy Valley, OR 97086. *(Kept to signed contracts only — not published on the website. Consider replacing with a PO box / registered-agent address before wide distribution so the home address isn't in every district's files — Doc 20 B. The DPA/NDPA uses this same notice address by reference.)*

**Exhibits:** (1) DPA / SDPC NDPA · (2) Order/Pricing · (3) Data elements schedule (NDPA Exhibit B, from `01_...`) · (4) Security overview / Trust page reference.

### Reviewer notes
- Sections **7, 12, 13** are where district procurement/counsel push hardest (data terms, liability cap for breaches, insurance). Have your attorney pre-negotiate your acceptable positions so you can turn deals around fast.
- Keep the MSA's data terms **identical in substance** to the NDPA so you're never maintaining two different privacy promises.
- This is the one document a district's legal team will most want to redline — a clean, reasonable starting draft speeds every deal.
