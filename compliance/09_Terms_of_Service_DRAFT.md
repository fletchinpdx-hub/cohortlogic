> Your live "terms" today is a **Beta Agreement** that instructs users **not to enter real student data** ("Use test or anonymized data only"). Going production reverses that: real student data is permitted **under contract**. This draft is the production ToS skeleton. For paid district relationships, the controlling contract is usually the **MSA (`10_...`)**; these ToS govern self-serve/website signups and incorporate the DPA and Privacy Policy by reference.

**3. Accounts and eligibility.** The Service is for school staff and administrators. You must provide accurate information, keep credentials secure, enable multi-factor authentication where offered, and are responsible for activity under your account. New accounts require administrator approval.

**6. Data protection and privacy.** Our handling of personal and student data is governed by the **Privacy Policy** and, where signed, a **Data Processing Agreement / SDPC NDPA**, each incorporated by reference. In a conflict for student data, the signed DPA/NDPA controls.

**9. Data return and deletion.** On termination, Customer may export its data, and we will delete it per the Privacy Policy retention schedule (deletion within 30 days of request; backups aged out within 90 days).
