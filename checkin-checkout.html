The problem

PBIS check-ins still live on paper and in spreadsheets.

Most schools run CICO programs with printed forms, spreadsheet logs, or notes passed between teachers. It works until you need to spot a trend, respond to a pattern, or show the district what's happening across 30 students.

✕

No building-wide visibility

Each teacher tracks their own students in isolation. Administrators have no real-time view of what's happening across the building.

✕

Trends are invisible until it's too late

Paper logs and scattered spreadsheets make it nearly impossible to see a student's trajectory week over week before a situation escalates.

✕

Reporting takes hours

Pulling data for a team meeting or a parent conversation means manually compiling check-in sheets that were never designed to be aggregated.

Features

📋

Period-by-period scoring

Incident logging

Common questions.

Who uses Check-in / Check-out? +
Primarily school administrators — principals and assistant principals — who need building-wide visibility into behavioral support. Teachers and counselors who conduct daily check-ins also use the entry view to log scores and incidents.
What is the scoring system? +
Students are scored on a 0–2 scale for each check-in period: 0 means needs support (shown in red), 1 means developing (amber), and 2 means meeting expectations (green). You can configure how many periods your school uses.
How do I add students? +
You can add students one at a time through the Students view, or import a roster from an Excel file. Students are associated with your school and only visible to staff at your school.
Is student data secure? +
Yes. Student data is encrypted in transit and at rest, and each school's data is strictly isolated — no other school can access it. Staff must be approved by your school admin before gaining access. See our security page for the full picture.
Is it really free? +
Yes. Check-in / Check-out is free for any school through September 2026. No credit card required. We'll give you advance notice before any pricing changes.
Honest note on security

We're not yet SOC 2 audited or part of formal DPA programs. We won't claim we are until we are. What we can say: student data is encrypted, isolated to your school, and never shared or sold. See the full picture →
