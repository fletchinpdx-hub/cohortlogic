The terms for testing Cohort Logic.

Cohort Logic is in beta. This is the short, plain-English agreement you accept when you create an account and help us test the product.

Version 1.0 — effective June 2026

Thank you for helping us test Cohort Logic. By creating an account and signing in, you agree to the following:

  1. 1. This is beta software.

    Cohort Logic is pre-release. Features may change, break, or be temporarily unavailable, and data could be lost. Please don't treat it as your system of record.

  2. 2. Use test or anonymized data only.

    During the beta, do not enter real, identifiable student information. Use initials, made-up names, or made-up test IDs.

  3. 3. We protect what you enter.

    We use industry-standard safeguards, including: encrypted connections, access controls, and strict per-school data isolation. We will never sell your data or share it for marketing.

  4. 4. Data retention.

    Beta data may be deleted at any time. You can request deletion of your school's data at any time, completed within 30 days. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

    We may contact you to ask about your experience.

    The beta is provided "as is," without warranties of any kind. We're not liable for any loss arising from use of the beta.
