How it works

From spreadsheet to balanced classes in four steps.

01

Bring your data in

Import from Excel or a public Google Sheet. Map your columns once. The app reads names, grades, and any scores or flags you care about.

02

Add the human stuff

Set constraints: keep these two apart, keep those two together, balance IEP students across rooms. The algorithm handles the rest.

03

Solve and review

One click generates balanced class lists. Drag students between classes to fine-tune. Every constraint violation is flagged immediately.

04

Export and decide

Download a clean Excel file with one tab per grade. Take it to your team. Nothing is stored on our servers when you're done.

Built differently

What we stand for.

⏻

Secure

Student data never leaves your browser. Nothing is stored on our servers. We'll tell you exactly where that changes, when it changes.

∑

Smart

The algorithm is deterministic and explainable. Every placement has a reason. No black-box surprises.

⌖

Student focused

Balance is about equity, not just convenience. IEP distribution, behavior considerations, sibling rules all count.

◎

Education built

Every decision is made with school leaders, not consultants. We build for the people who will actually use it.

Honest note on security

We're not yet SOC 2 audited or part of formal DPA programs. We won't claim we are until we are. What we can say: student data runs entirely in your browser and is never sent to our servers. That's the design, not the promise.
