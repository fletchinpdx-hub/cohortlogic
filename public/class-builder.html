The problem

Placement is the hardest decision most principals make. It deserves better than sticky notes on a wall.

Class lists carry hundreds of constraints (teacher requests, parent notes, behavioral dynamics, IEPs, ELL targets, gender balance, friend groups) and most of them live in someone's head. The result is a long, painful process, and decisions that can be hard to defend in August.

What we actually do We don't make the decisions for you. We do the bookkeeping (tracking every rule, doing the balance math, holding your pins in place) so the judgement that belongs to you stays the judgement that belongs to you.
How the Planner works

What's in the box

The features that earn the work.

Keep-apart & keep-together

Flag the pairs who shouldn't share a room and the pairs who should. The solver places every roster around those rules.

Keep-with-teacher pins

Pin a student to a specific teacher or classroom. Those pins hold through every regenerate, so a deliberate placement never gets shuffled away.

Balance any group

Set an even spread across gender, ethnicity, IEP, ELL, or any category you define, and the solver distributes them across rooms.

Even academic split

Balance reading, math, or behavior scores so no class ends up loaded. Weight the fields that matter most to your building.

Drag to adjust

Not happy with a placement? Drag any student to another room. The solver did the heavy lifting; you always make the final call.

Runs in your browser

Your roster is read and balanced entirely on your device. Nothing is uploaded, nothing is stored on our servers, and we never train models on it.

Common questions

What principals always ask first.

How long does setup take? +
It depends on your school's size and how clean your roster data is. We'll sit with you on a setup call for the first import. That's included.
Does this integrate with my SIS? +
Class Builder works from a CSV or Excel export from your SIS or a Google Sheet, and we'll help you map the columns. A direct SIS connector isn't something we've built. If it would matter to your team, tell us — that's how we decide what to build next.
What if the algorithm gets it wrong? +
You're not bound to the solver's output. Every placement is editable, and the solver will respect your manual pins on the next run. Cohort Logic is a tool for your judgement, not a replacement for it.
Who sees student data? +
Class Builder doesn't save or store the data you bring in. We don't train models on rosters, ever. Read more on the security page, and please anonymize where you can (use student IDs instead of names).
How much does it cost? +
Free for any school through September 2026. We'll publish honest pricing per school as we ship more products, and notify you well in advance of any change.
