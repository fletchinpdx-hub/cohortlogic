The problem

Placement is the hardest decision most principals make. It deserves better than sticky notes on a wall.

Class lists carry hundreds of constraints (teacher requests, parent notes, behavioral dynamics, IEPs, ELL targets, gender balance, friend groups) and most of them live in someone's head. The result is a long, painful process, and decisions that can be hard to defend in August.

What we actually do We don't make the decisions for you. We do the bookkeeping (the constraint tracking, the tradeoff math, the version history) so the judgement that belongs to you stays the judgement that belongs to you.

What's in the box

The features that earn the work.

Parallel scenarios

Spin up two or three placement scenarios with different teacher requests prioritized, different balance targets, and compare them constraint by constraint.

Rationale per student

Every student card shows which rules influenced their placement. Defend any decision later with the original context, not from memory.

Sibling & cohort linking

Keep siblings on the same special schedule. Keep peer groups stable across grade levels. The model remembers.

Teacher load view

Live view of each teacher's roster: IEPs, behavior flags, ELL count, and which parent requests they ended up with.

Audit log

Every change, and every solve, is timestamped and attributable. When a question comes up next April, you have the answer.

Common questions

What principals always ask first.

How long does setup take? +
It depends on your school's size and how clean your roster data is. We'll sit with you on a setup call for the first import. That's included.
Does this integrate with my SIS? +
Today, Class Builder works from a CSV. We'll help you map the fields. Direct sync with PowerSchool, Infinite Campus, and Skyward is on the roadmap.
What if the algorithm gets it wrong? +
You're not bound to the solver's output. Every placement is editable, and the solver will respect your manual pins on the next run. Cohort Logic is a tool for your judgement, not a replacement for it.
Who sees student data? +
Class Builder doesn't save or store the data you bring in. We don't train models on rosters, ever. Read more on the security page, and please anonymize where you can (use student IDs instead of names).
How much does it cost? +
Free for any school through September 2026. We'll publish honest pricing per school as we ship more products, and notify you well in advance of any change.
