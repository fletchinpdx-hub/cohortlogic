How we build

A short, honest loop.

We're a small team. We move slowly on purpose. We'd rather get a few things right than a lot of things halfway.

01

Talk to schools

We start every product by spending real time with the principals, APs, and counselors who'd use it. No surveys, no decks.

02

Build the smallest thing

We ship the simplest version that solves a real problem. We'd rather have one feature you love than ten you tolerate.

03

Ship, listen, repeat

Every release is shaped by what early schools tell us. If something isn't working, it's our job to fix it, not yours to work around.

What we won't compromise

Four constraints, on every product we ship.

These come from the brand we're building, and from watching too many edtech tools get them wrong. Every feature has to clear all four.

01 · Secure Privacy by default. We don't train models on your students, ever.
02 · Smart A solver that shows its work. Every decision has an explanation.
03 · Student focused Decisions that survive the parent meeting in August.
04 · Education built Designed with school leaders, not adapted from a generic tool.
