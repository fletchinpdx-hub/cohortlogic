Which file is which?

Three files, three different jobs. Only one of them is your work.

📄 Your roster.xlsx / .csv
  • This is your source data — the student list you already keep in your SIS or a spreadsheet.
  • It only comes in. Class Builder reads it in your browser; it is never sent to a server.
  • Editing the original later won't update anything here. Upload it again if it changes.
  • Don't have one? Download the template or the sample below.
📘 Your work file.cohortlogic
  • This is your work. It is the only file you can open again and keep editing.
  • Save it before you close this tab. Class Builder keeps nothing — close without saving and your roster, rules, and classes are gone.
  • Keep it somewhere safe — your school's Google Drive or OneDrive, not just the Downloads folder.
  • Email it to a colleague and they can carry on exactly where you stopped.
  • Schedule Builder reads it too — your school name and staff carry across.
📗 Class lists.xlsx
  • This is the finished product — ready to read, print, and hand out.
  • Opens in Excel or Google Sheets. One tab per grade, or one tab per teacher.
  • Share it with staff or print it for a binder.
  • It is a one-way copy. You cannot load it back in — anything you change there will not come back.

The short version: your roster goes in. The .cohortlogic file is your original — keep it. The .xlsx is the printout — share it. If you only save one file, save the .cohortlogic.
